How to Play

You have to discover cells from the board. You can discover an empty piece which will be spread until reach numbers, or you can discover directly a number or a mine. In the last case the game is finished and you loose.

The numbers meaning are regarding the number of mines around that cell.

You can press right button on a cell to mark it with a flag when you discover a mine there.

The game is finished when you have flagged correctly all of the mines.
